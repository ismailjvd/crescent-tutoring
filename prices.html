HIGH-QUALITY SERVICES AT A REASONABLE PRICE

One of our main goals at Crescent Tutoring is to provide a high quality, in-depth learning experience at a reasonable price. Too often, we have seen companies charge outrageous fees that students and their families simply can’t afford.


That’s where we come in. Our highly trained staff with years of tutoring experience prioritizes the success of our clients over anything else. As a growing local business with small marketing and leasing expenses, we can offer excellent services for a fraction of the price of our competitors.

FREE TWO HOUR SESSION
To demonstrate how committed we are to your success, we are willing to offer your first session, up to 2 hours, absolutely free of charge. This way, you can see for yourself the results of one session and decide whether our services are right for you. By giving us a try, there’s nothing to lose.

RATES
Rather than having a flat-rate that we apply to all clients, we take the following factors into consideration
  • Frequency and length of sessions
  • Location
  • Difficulty of subject
  • Extra resources and materials
Depending on these factors, our rates can be as low as $15 per hour of one-on-one tutoring.

FINANCIAL NEED
We understand that there are students with difficult financial circumstances. Financial troubles should not be a reason that a student is barred from achieving their goals. For this reason, students can fill out a supplementary form demonstrating and describing their financial need. After evaluation of these documents, we can determine whether a student may receive a special discounted rate for our services.
